What happened to Instagram?

Instagram claimed to be the app to replace Facebook and stood for casual photo dumps. Multiple users have complained about the number of ads and sponsored posts that appear on their feeds. By making such controversial updates, Instagram has lost its originality and many of its users. While I certainly agree with these users, I believe Instagram is here to stay even though most reports indicate otherwise.
